Synopsis

Two imprisoned men bond over a number of years, finding solace and eventual redemption through acts of common decency. Andy Dufresne, a successful banker, is arrested for the murders of his wife and her lover, and is sentenced to life imprisonment at the Shawshank prison. He becomes the most unconventional prisoner and befriends Red, a fellow convict. This is a story of hope, friendship, and perseverance against all odds.
